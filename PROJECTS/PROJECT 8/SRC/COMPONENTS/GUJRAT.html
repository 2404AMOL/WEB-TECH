Chevdo/chewdo – Kenya style Recipe ia s Gujarat snack mix with rice flakes, potato crisps, lentils and nuts usually made at celebrations like Diwali. There are lots of variations of chevdo and Kenya chevdo has it’s own unique taste. Kenyan’s like to eat the chevdo with onions and lemon or with the Kenyan tomato sauce which again is an acquired taste.
